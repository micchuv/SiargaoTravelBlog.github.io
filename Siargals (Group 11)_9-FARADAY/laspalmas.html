Las Palmas Villas and Casitas

Sighted in the 16th century, Siargao Island was first known as Isla de las Palmas. As an ode to the origin of the island, we decided to name the resort Las Palmas Villas & Casitas. The name perfectly pays homage to the past while capturing the beauty of the present day. We took the inspiration of the island’s beauty and charm into our resort. We invite you to experience tranquility in the tropics with us.

ROOMS

The accommodations of the resort bring the beauty of Siargao’s surroundings into one’s own comfort indoors. This is why you get a glimpse of the sky and coconut trees in your bed. And why you can enjoy the outdoors in the privacy of your own room.

The materials and finished used evoke the natural, beautiful materials that we see outdoors. From the thatched roofing, beautiful tropical wooden furniture, the rooms in Las Palmas Villas and Casitas carry with them the beauty and tranquility of the island.
